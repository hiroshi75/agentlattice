# Call Prep

Get ready for any sales meeting in minutes. Combines account research, relationship context, and meeting-type-specific preparation into one actionable brief.

## How It Works

**Standalone Mode**: Provide company name, meeting type, and attendees. Web research fills in the context.

**Supercharged Mode**: Connect CRM, email, chat, calendar, or transcript tools for automatic context pulling.

## Getting Started

- "Prep me for my call with Acme Corp"
- "Get me ready for my demo with Stripe tomorrow"
- "Meeting prep for my discovery call with the Notion team"

## Output Format

### Account Snapshot
Company overview, deal stage, key metrics, relationship status.

### Attendee Profiles
For each attendee: name, title, background, LinkedIn, prior interactions, talking points.

### Interaction History (if CRM connected)
Timeline of recent touches, open commitments, unresolved items.

### Recent News
Company announcements relevant to the conversation, with talking points.

### Suggested Agenda
Tailored to meeting type with time allocations:

**Discovery Call**: Rapport building, situation questions, pain exploration, impact assessment, next steps.

**Demo**: Recap priorities, tailored walkthrough, specific use cases, Q&A, next steps.

**Negotiation**: Value recap, address concerns, terms discussion, mutual action plan.

**Check-in**: Review progress, gather feedback, identify expansion, plan next steps.

### Discovery Questions
Targeted questions to fill gaps in understanding, organized by topic.

### Anticipated Objections
Common objections for this stage and account, with recommended responses.

### Internal Notes
Competitive intelligence, pricing guidance, relevant case studies.

## Tips

- More context = better prep. Share prior notes, emails, or specific concerns.
- Specify the meeting type for tailored preparation.
- Ask for follow-up on specific areas after initial prep.
